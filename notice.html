NOTICE

GEETANJALI ENGLISH

LITERARY COMPETITION

SESSION 2024-25

GEETANJALI ENGLISH LITERARY SOCIETY is pleased to announce the English Literary Competition for students of BSc, BCA, MSc, and MA English Literature. The competition will be organized by the Department of English and will take place on:

Date: 18th February 2025       Time: 12:00 NOON onwards      Venue: Room Number 210

Competitions:

1. Essay Writing
Event incharge - Dr. Sankalp Jogi
Topic: "The Significance of Guru-Shishya Bonding in the Gurukul System of Education: A Timeless Tradition"
Word limit: 500 words
The topic explores the importance of the guru-shishya relationship in the traditional Indian education system, where the teacher-student bond is considered sacred and essential for the transmission of knowledge and values.
2. Poster Competition
Event incharge - Ms. Shivali Rai
Topic: Related to Indian Knowledge Tradition, including religious and value-based scriptures, characters that motivate life skills, etc. Students are encouraged to create posters that reflect the richness and diversity of Indian Knowledge Tradition.
3. Quiz Competition
Event incharge- Mrs. Rachna Thakur
Mode: On the spot
The quiz will test students' knowledge of Indian Knowledge Tradition, including literature, history, philosophy, and culture.
4. Picture Composition
Event incharge - Dr. Usha Masram
Mode: On the spot
Students will be given a picture related to Indian Knowledge Tradition and will be asked to compose a short write-up (around 200-250 words) based on the picture.
Eligibility: BSc, BCA, MSc, and MA English Literature students
Registration Deadline: 15th February 2025
Contact: Respective event incharge or Department of English

Note:
1. Students can register their names with the respective teacher in-charge.
2. The Poster and Essay to be submitted on the mentioned date to the event Incharge.
